# EmenTech SEO Strategy

This document outlines the comprehensive SEO strategy for EmenTech, a digital agency specializing in web development, AI/ML solutions, and mobile application development. The strategy targets both local Kenya market and international clients seeking quality software development services.

### Primary Audience
- Business owners in Kenya seeking digital transformation
- Startups looking for MVP development
- Established companies needing custom software solutions
- International businesses outsourcing development to Kenya

### Secondary Audience

- Technology partners and collaborators
